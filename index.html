Exciting New Additions to Our Collection!

August 16, 2024

We are thrilled to announce a fresh wave of new arrivals at our library! From best-selling novels to insightful non-fiction, our shelves are stocked with the latest must-reads across various genres.


Top Picks:

  1. "Echoes of Eternity" by Amanda Steele: A thrilling sci-fi adventure that’s taking the world by storm.
  2. "The Mindful Path" by Dr. Lila Owens: A deep dive into mindfulness and well-being, perfect for anyone looking to find balance in their busy lives.
  3. "Under the Midnight Sun" by Hiroshi Shimizu: A haunting mystery novel set in the snowy landscapes of northern Japan.

Events to Look Forward To:

Author Talk with Amanda Steele: Join us on August 25th at 3 PM for an exclusive live session with the author of "Echoes of Eternity."

Book Club Revival: Our monthly book club is back! Starting September 1st, we’ll be discussing "The Mindful Path."

Children’s Storytime: Every Saturday at 10 AM, bring your little ones for an engaging storytelling session featuring beloved classics and new favorites.


Community Picks:

Our community’s favorite read this month is "The Lost Gardens" by Emily Hughes. Dive into this enchanting tale of discovery and magic.

Visit our library today to explore these exciting new titles and more! Don’t forget to check out our digital library for e-books and audiobooks available for download.

Stay Connected: Follow us on social media for daily updates, event announcements, and book recommendations.
